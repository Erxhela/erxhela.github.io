Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") describes how Dream Indoor Playground ("Dream") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards the personal information of visitors to its premises and users of its services. By accessing or using Dream, you consent to the practices described in this Policy.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Personal Information: Dream may collect personal information, such as names, contact details, and identification documents, when visitors voluntarily provide such information during the admission process or when using Dream's services.

1.2 Children's Information: Dream may collect personal information of children, such as names, ages, and emergency contact details, with the consent of their parents or legal guardians. Dream only collects personal information of children to ensure their safety and well-being while using the facilities.

1.3 Non-Personal Information: Dream may collect non-personal information, such as demographic data, preferences, and usage patterns, through website analytics, cookies, or similar technologies. This information helps improve Dream's services and enhance the user experience.

2. Use of Information

2.1 Dream may use the collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage access to Dream's facilities and services.
  • To communicate with visitors and respond to inquiries or requests.
  • To process payments for admission fees or other services.
  • To ensure the safety and security of visitors and their children.
  • To improve Dream's facilities, services, and user experience.
  • To inform visitors about updates, promotions, or special events related to Dream (with opt-out options available).
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce the terms and conditions.

2.2 Dream does not sell or rent personal information to third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law or as necessary to provide the requested services.

3. Disclosure of Information

3.1 Dream may disclose personal information in the following circumstances:

  • To authorized employees, contractors, or service providers who assist in operating Dream's facilities or providing services.
  • To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government regulations.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Dream, its visitors, or others.

3.2 Dream may also disclose non-personal information in an aggregated or de-identified form for marketing, research, or analytical purposes.

4. Data Security

4.1 Dream employs reasonable security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, or alteration. However, no data transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and Dream cannot guarantee the absolute security of personal information.

4.2 Visitors must adhere to the rules and regulations posted within the premises of Dream. Failure to comply with the rules may result in immediate expulsion from Dream without refund.

5. Third-Party Links

5.1 Dream's website or services may contain links to third-party websites or services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to such third-party sites, and Dream is not responsible for their privacy practices. Visitors are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these third-party sites before providing any personal information.

6. Children's Privacy

6.1 Dream takes special care to protect the privacy and safety of children. Dream does not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. Parents or legal guardians may review, request modification, or deletion of their child's personal information by contacting Dream.

7. Access and Control

7.1 Visitors may review, update, correct, or request the deletion of their personal information by contacting Dream. Dream will make reasonable efforts to accommodate such requests, subject to any legal obligations or legitimate business purposes.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

8.1 Dream may update or modify this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective upon posting the revised Policy on Dream's website. Visitors are encouraged to review this Policy periodically to stay informed about how Dream collects, uses, and protects personal information.
